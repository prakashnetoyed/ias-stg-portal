    Select the Membership That Matches You

    Members posted in NCT of Delhi shall pay a tenure membership fee of ₹1000 per posting in Govt. of India.

    Other Members of the association posted in the NCR Delhi shall pay an annual membership free of Rs.200 .

    Retired members shall pay a one-time life membership fee of Rs.500.
