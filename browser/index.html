    MEMBERSHIP CATEGORIES

    Select the Membership That Matches You

    Members of the Service posted at NCT of Delhi shall pay a tenure membership fee of ₹1000 per tenure posting in Government of India

    Other members of the Association posted in the NCT of Delhi shall pay an annual membership fee of ₹200

    Retired members shall pay a one-time life membership fee of ₹500.
