    Lifetime Membership

    Indian Civil & Administrative Service (Central) Association, New Delhi

    Please download Life Membership Application Form of the Indian Civil & Administrative Service (Central) Association, New Delhiwith the request to take a print (only first 2 pages– Ignore the 3 page) of the attachment.

    Download link for   ApplicationForm-IC&AS(C)A  After filling it completely, please send it to the Hony. Treasurer along with the LifeMembership fee of Rs.5000/- through cheque / demand draft / RTGS/NEFT.

    Click here to know How to Pay the Membership Fee

    Please reioned at the end of the Application Form carefully before filling the Application Form. You are also requested to please circulate in your Batch groups and Cadre groups for which I would be personally grateful. May I also request that if there is any member of the IAS in your Office / Building / Department / Organisation or anybody whose in your contact then you may please handover a photocopy of the first two pages of the Application Form with the request that he/she may kindly do the needful at the earliest. Those who have submitted their Application along with the membership fee, they need not send their application form again but they are requested to help the Association in getting the membership forms filled by their batch-mates and cadre-mates.

    For more information about membership benefits, Get in touch with us.
