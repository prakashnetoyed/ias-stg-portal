    Refund & Cancellation Policy

    At the Indian Administrative Service Central Association (IASCA), we value the commitment of our members who choose to be part of our community. We strive to maintain transparency in all our processes, including payments, memberships, and welfare contributions.

    Membership Payments

    • IASCA offers three membership categories. Once a membership is purchased and payment is successfully processed, it is considered final and non-refundable.
    • Memberships cannot be cancelled, transferred, or adjusted after payment.
    • No partial or prorated refunds will be provided for early termination of membership.

    Welfare Fund Contributions

    • Contributions made towards the Welfare Fund are entirely voluntary.
    • All such contributions are non-refundable and non-cancellable, regardless of the mode of payment.
    • Once acknowledged, welfare fund contributions cannot be withdrawn or claimed back under any circumstances.

    Event Registrations (if applicable)

    • In case of event participation fees, once registered and paid, the amount will be treated as final and non-refundable.

    Exceptional Cases

    Refunds or cancellations will not be entertained under normal circumstances. However, in case of any technical error during payment processing (such as double payment deduction), members are requested to contact us immediately at association.ias@gmail.com with payment details. Valid cases will be reviewed and resolved accordingly.
